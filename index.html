General information

The Society for Continuing Education (SCE) is a non-governmental organization established in 1998. The organisation represents around 25 professionals – administrators and experts in the field of education training (vocational, higher, continuing and adult education), including university professors, officials from research organisations and employment services, local government officials, practicing lawyers. Through its expert networks the organization has well established links with a broad spectrum of stakeholders in public, private and non-governmental sector. Apart from the projects implemented by SCE, its individual members have a substantial experience and project implementation history through their affiliation to other organisations (universities and other public and private organisations).
